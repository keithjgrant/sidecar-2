{{< ingredients
  "1½ oz Campari"
  "1½ oz sweet vermouth"
  "1½ oz sparkling wine"
  "orange peel spiral"
>}}


{{< instructions >}}
Stir together the Campari and sweet vermouth with ice. Strain into a champagne flute. Top with sparkling wine.

Garnish with a thin orange spiral.
