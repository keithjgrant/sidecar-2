{{< wrap >}}
{{< summary >}}
If you’re feeling extra adventurous, muddle one or two slices of jalapeño in your shaker before adding the ingredients.
{{< /summary >}}


{{< ingredients
  "2 oz tequila"
  "¾ oz orange liqueur"
  "¾ oz lime juice"
  "¼ oz agave syrup"
  "1 pinch of salt"
>}}


{{< instructions >}}
Shake vigorously with ice. Strain into a rocks glass filled with fresh ice.
{{< /instructions >}}
