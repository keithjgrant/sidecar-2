{{< wrap >}}
{{< ingredients
  "2 oz vodka"
  "½ oz Campari"
  "1½ oz grapefruit juice"
  "½ oz grenadine"
  "grapefruit wedge"
>}}


{{< instructions >}}
Shake vodka, Campari, grapefruit juice, and grenadine. Strain into rocks glass over ice.

Garnish with grapefruit wedge.
{{< /instructions >}}
