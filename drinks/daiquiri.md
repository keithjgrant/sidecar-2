---
title: "Daiquiri"
date: "2018-01-15T11:17:51-05:00"
glass: coupe
sweetness: 2
booziness: 2
family: sour
ingredients: [
  "2 oz white rum",
  "¾ oz lime juice",
  "¾ oz simple syrup"
]
tags: [
  "rum",
  "lime",
  "classic-cocktail",
  "shaken"
]
---
Add all ingredients to a cocktail shaker with plenty of ice. Shake well and strain into a coupe glass.
