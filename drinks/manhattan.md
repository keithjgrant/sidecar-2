---
title: "Manhattan"
date: 2018-01-13T11:31:10-05:00
glass: coupe
sweetness: 1
booziness: 3
family: martini
ingredients: [
  "2 oz rye whiskey",
  "1 oz sweet vermouth",
  "2 dashes Angostura bitters",
]
garnish: maraschino cherry
tags: [
  "classic-cocktail",
  "rye-whiskey",
  "sweet-vermouth",
  "stirred"
]

Combine in a shaker with ice. Stir, then strain into a coupe glass.

Garnish with a Maraschino cherry.
